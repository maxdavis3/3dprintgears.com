Gear Printing Services

01

Polymer Gear Prototypes

Nylon PA12 (SLS) and POM (FDM) gear prototypes for kinematic testing and design validation. True involute tooth form at module 0.5 and above. Quieter operation than metal-on-metal — ideal for low-noise drive trains.

02

Metal Gear Production

SLM 316L stainless and 17-4 PH steel gears for high-torque applications where polymer cannot survive. Integral hubs, keyways, and mounting features in a single print. Post-heat treatment to AGMA mechanical requirements.

03

Custom Compound Gear Assemblies

Compound gear trains, planetary stages, and epicyclic assemblies printed as near-complete assemblies. Integrated bearing seats, lubricant channels, and clearance fits dimensioned for as-printed assembly without secondary machining.

GEAR MATERIALS

Material Options by Application

Gear material selection governs noise, load, speed, and service life — our portfolio covers the full range.

  • ● Nylon PA12 (SLS) — low-noise, self-lubricating
  • ● POM (Acetal) — stiff, chemical resistant, low friction
  • ● 316L stainless — corrosion resistant, food-grade
  • ● 17-4 PH steel — hardened, high-torque applications
  • ● PEEK — high-temperature, aggressive chemical environments
  • ● Bronze-filled nylon — bearing-surface wear resistance
